MISSION & VISION

VISION The Joyce Vocational Institute, as the Premier Institution of Learning for Vocational Technical Education continues to mold its learners for entrepreneurial and livelihood opportunities, as well as college preparation, with strong support from committed teachers and community stakeholders, thus developing them into morally upright, socially mobile citizens and God-loving individuals. MISSION In alignment with the Division Educational Development Plan, it commits to provide the students with effective academic instruction, relevant technical vocational training and socio – moral formation through the Strengthened Secondary Technical Vocational Education under the Basic Education Curriculum and in strong linkage with all other stakeholders. OBJECTIVES To expose students to effective classroom management for the acquisition of knowledge. To make the youth feel a genuine love for study and work. To instill in the students deep spiritual and ethical values necessary in the development of respect and goodwill for each other. To uphold the ideals of nationalism, democracy and family through planned academic disciplines, which inculcate in the youth’s participative awareness on government peace and reconciliation thrusts. To help indigent but deserving students achieve high level accomplishment through scholarship grants. To help teachers and staff grow professionally and boost their morale for high productivity through staff development program. To forge links with the community to ensure success in the holistic molding of the youth. To imbibe in the students the appreciation of and concern for the preservation of the environment and natural resources THE DEPED VISION We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation. As a learner-centered public institution, the Department of Education continuously improves itself to better serve its stakeholders. THE DEPED MISSION To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based, and complete basic education where: Students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment. Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner. Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen. Family, community, and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners. OUR CORE VALUES Maka-Diyos Maka-tao Makakalikasan Makabansa
